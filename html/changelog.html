11 November 2020

Jamini updated:

  • Renamed and removed Oberth Republic gear from spawn pools
  • fixed TCS

Toriate updated:

  • Defib timer is now 15 minutes again, as it was
  • Roundstart spawnpoints should now function again, instead of randomly dumping you in maintenance

10 November 2020

CEV-Eris Developers updated:

  • NPC monkeys don't receive environment sanity damage and insight.
  • onLife() sanity procs effects trigger once every 2 life ticks.

Jamini updated:

  • Added disk compartmentalizer
  • open panel overlay on smartfridges now goes away when you close the panel!
  • fixed pipe dispenser UI
  • fixed disposal dispenser UI
  • fixed custom tables being unable to be deconstructed

Toriate updated:

  • Ported sofas from Polaris
  • Ported a bunch of underwear options.
  • Ported a ton more patches from upstream
  • You can now throw grenades up/down ladders, as well as look up and down them with alt click
  • Helmets will keep you from being stunned by small items falling from a higher Z level
  • Having a high MEC stat will allow you to determine whether a battery is rigged to explode or not when examined.
  • All .35 pistols aside from the Giskard can now use high capacity magazines
  • Fixed some minor bugs regarding the Brigador and Svallin slot flags.
  • Fixed a bug that allowed duplicating stacks of money.

09 November 2020

Toriate updated:

  • Ported yet another update to church cloning

01 November 2020

Jamini updated:

  • A less-suspicious turtleneck
  • Laserpointers
  • tennis balls
  • added a number of objects to loadout lists
  • added ability to have icon path transforms for the mask slot
  • bananas (the mask ones) now fit and display on the face and head. (Helmet bananas do not do this currently!)
  • banking card fixes
  • reduced autolathe spawn rate
  • autolathes can no longer recycle anything at 100% efficiency
  • buffed smelter recycling rate when upgraded from 5% per tier to 15% per tier.

Toriate updated:

  • Ported Polaris translators
